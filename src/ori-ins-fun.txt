the major muscles of respiration

------muscles of the abdominal cavity
------------
------------

------------

 ~
external oblique

unilateral action - lateral flexion of the vertebral column:
                    rotation of vertebral column to opposite side
bilateral action - flexes vertebral column and compresses abdominal wall:
                    used in forced exhalation

 ~
internal oblique

unilateral action - lateral flexion of the vertebral column:
                     rotation of vertebral column to same side
bilateral action - flexes vertebral column and compresses abdominal wall:
                    used in forced exhalation

 ~
transverse abdominus

bilater action - compresses abdominal wall used in forced exhalation
